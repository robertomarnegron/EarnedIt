Support

Help, bug reports, and feature ideas

EarnedIt is a one-person project, so "support" is just me. The best way to reach me is email. I usually reply within a couple of days.

Email: admin@ron-media.com

Before you email

Most issues fall into one of three buckets. If one of these matches, the fix might be faster than waiting on a reply.

"My count is stuck at zero."

  • Check that you granted HealthKit access in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → EarnedIt and that Active Energy is turned on.
  • Check that your iPhone actually has active energy samples today. Open the Health app → Browse → Active Energy.
  • If you're using the simulator, HealthKit has no data by default. Seed it via Health app → Active Energy → Add Data.

"The Watch doesn't show my favorites."

  • Open the iPhone app once after you star or unstar a craving, so the Watch receives the update.
  • Make sure your iPhone and Apple Watch are paired and on the same Apple ID.
  • Custom cravings with photos are iPhone-only for now. The Watch shows built-in cravings.

"My custom craving photo looks weird."

Custom craving photos are center-cropped to a 512×512 square. Very tall or very wide photos get cropped tighter than you might expect — try re-adding with the subject closer to the middle.

Reporting a bug

When you email, it really helps if you include:

  • What you were doing when it happened.
  • What you expected vs. what you got.
  • Your iPhone model and iOS version, and Watch model if relevant.
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if you can.
  • Whether it happens every time or just once.

Feature ideas

Yes please. If you want a specific food, drink, cultural snack, or calorie value added, send me the name and an estimated kcal-per-item and I'll consider it for a future build. You can also add anything you want as a custom craving right now.
